MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

Date: 1 June 2021
Client: Alpha & Partners LLP ("Client")
Service Provider: Nexus Legal Technologies Ltd ("Nexus")

1. SERVICES
Nexus shall provide AI-powered contract review services as described in Schedule 1.

2. FEES
Client shall pay Nexus £8,000 per month for the duration of this agreement.

3. TERM AND TERMINATION
This agreement shall commence on 1 June 2021 and continue for an initial term of 3 years.
Either party may terminate with 90 days' written notice after the initial term.

4. CHANGE OF CONTROL
In the event of a change of control of Nexus, Client shall have the right to terminate this agreement
with 30 days' written notice. "Change of control" means any transaction resulting in a new entity
acquiring more than 50% of the voting shares of Nexus.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality over all materials exchanged under this agreement.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.

Signed: Sarah Mitchell, Managing Partner, Alpha & Partners LLP
Signed: Marcus Webb, CEO, Nexus Legal Technologies Ltd
